Who are we?

Monica's Auction is the result of the Full Stack Frameworks with Django Milestone Project made by Monica Rasquin. It represents in a simple way how an Auction house works. The whole back & front end was designed and build by Rasquin. All the content material here presented, including artifacts, prices, pictures, information and won auction is fictional.

Some condition issues may not be noted in the condition report but are apparent in the provided photos which are considered part of the condition report.
